Welcome to The Fat Duck!

Your perfect dining experience begins here.

Meet the Owner...

Frederik Andersen, the visionary owner, has always had a passion for food and history. After years of traveling the world and exploring different culinary traditions, Frederik decided to open The Fat Duck to share his love for hearty, flavorful meals made from locally sourced ingredients. He believes in the power of fire, fat, and taste in bringing people together.

...and the Chef

Charlotte Jensen, our head chef, brings her years of experience in fine dining and rustic cooking to The Fat Duck. A true artisan in the kitchen, Charlotte uses her mastery of traditional techniques to create dishes that are rich in flavor and deeply satisfying. From braising to roasting, she believes in letting the ingredients speak for themselves.

Our Historical Building

The Fat Duck is housed in a beautifully restored 18th-century building located in the heart of the old town of Copenhagen. The building itself has a rich history, having been home to various merchants and artisans throughout the centuries.

We’ve worked hard to maintain its historic charm while offering a modern and comfortable dining experience. As you dine, you’ll feel the echoes of the past and the vibrant energy of Copenhagen’s bustling historic district. Every corner of The Fat Duck tells a story, from the wooden beams to the original stone walls, offering a truly unique setting to enjoy a luxurious meal.
